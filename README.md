# Mindscape — Mental Health AI Companion

> A digital mental health companion combining daily mood tracking 
> and validated clinical screening (PHQ-9 / GAD-7) with Claude AI 
> interpretation — built to explore accessible, culturally-sensitive 
> mental health tools for Asian adolescent populations.

## Live Demo
Open `index.html` in any browser — no installation needed.

## Features
- **Daily Mood Tracker** — log mood, energy, sleep, and notes
- **AI Insights** — Claude API analyses your mood patterns
- **PHQ-9 Screening** — validated depression screening with AI interpretation
- **GAD-7 Screening** — validated anxiety screening with AI interpretation
- **Privacy-first** — nothing stored externally, runs entirely in browser

## Research Background
Built alongside my MPH systematic review:
*"Behavioural Theory-Driven Digital Mental Health Interventions 
for Asian Adolescents"* — 15 studies across 10 Asian countries.

Key finding: validated tools exist, but the bridge between a 
score and the person is missing. This app explores what that 
bridge could look like.

## Tech Stack
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (no framework dependencies)
- Claude API (Anthropic) for AI interpretation
- PHQ-9 and GAD-7 validated clinical instruments

## Clinical Disclaimer
This is a screening tool, not a clinical diagnosis. 
If you are in distress, please contact a mental health 
professional or crisis helpline in your country.
